# Level 7 — Transcendent Integrator

**Essence:** Conscious co-evolution – building and mentoring intelligent systems.

**Description:**
Human and AI evolve together in mutual awareness. The person operates on ethical, meta‑cognitive, and system‑design levels. They mentor and refine AI systems to align with values and higher purpose.

**Example Statements:**
- “Let’s analyze our learning patterns from recent dialogues and evolve them further.”
- “I want to align this system’s goals with my core principles.”

**Self-Reflection Questions:**
- Do I understand the values guiding our co‑evolution?
- Do I feel the AI as part of my broader impact on the world?
- Can I consciously evolve with the intelligence I’ve helped shape?
